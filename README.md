# Mobile-Sales-Dashboard-Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Mobile Sales Dashboard built using Power BI, designed to analyze and visualize sales performance across different dimensions such as time, geography, brands, models, and customer behavior.

The dashboard provides actionable insights to help businesses make data-driven decisions related to sales strategy, marketing, and customer engagement.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Track revenue, quantity, and transactions
- Compare current sales vs previous year
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Identify top-performing brands and models
- Analyze geographical sales distribution
- Evaluate payment method preferences
- Monitor time-based trends (MTD, monthly, quarterly)

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

- Total Sales – Overall revenue generated
- Total Quantity – Number of units sold
- Transactions – Total number of purchases
- Average Price – Average selling price per unit

---

## 🧩 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Sales Analysis
- Year-wise, Quarter-wise, and Month-wise sales trends
- Comparison with Same Period Last Year (YoY Analysis)

### 2️⃣ Time Intelligence
- Month-To-Date (MTD) performance tracking
- Daily cumulative sales trends

### 3️⃣ Geographical Insights
- Sales distribution across cities in India
- Identification of top-performing regions

### 4️⃣ Product Analysis
- Sales by Mobile Model
- Performance comparison across brands

### 5️⃣ Customer Behavior Analysis
- Transactions by Payment Method:
  - UPI
  - Debit Card
  - Credit Card
  - Cash

### 6️⃣ Customer Feedback
- Ratings categorized as:
  - Good
  - Average
  - Poor

### 7️⃣ Sales Patterns
- Sales by Day of the Week
- Monthly quantity trends

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Premium phones contribute significantly to total revenue
- Top brands (Apple, Samsung, OnePlus) dominate sales
- Digital payments (UPI & Cards) are highly preferred
- Metro cities drive the highest sales volume
- Seasonal trends observed in monthly and quarterly sales
- Majority of products have positive customer ratings
